THE COFFEE SHOP




Awash in light on even the greyest days, our coffee house is the perfect place to settle in with a cup of coffee. Here you'll find expertly crafted espresso and batch brews, teas from Song and Harbor Herbalist, and pastries from our neighbors at The London Plane. Stop by and see what's good.

Espresso

Strong black coffee made by forcing steam through dark-roast aromatic coffee beans at high pressure in an espresso machine. A perfectly brewed espresso will have a thick, golden-brown crema (foam) on the surface.

Cappuccino

Combination of equal parts espresso, steamed milk and milk froth. This luxurious drink, if made properly, can double as a dessert with its complex flavors and richness.

Americano

a single shot of espresso added to a cup of hot water. A creamy, rich espresso-based coffee that you can sip and savor

Caf Mocha

a cappuccino or a caffe latte with chocolate syrup or powder added. There can be wide variations in exactly how this is prepared, so ask us how we do it before you order.
